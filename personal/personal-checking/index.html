PERSONAL CHECKING TO FIT YOUR LIFE

It’s like one-size-fits-all never happened

 

When it comes to personal checking, you need a solution tailored to fit your needs. Bank Iowa offers four attractive checking options so you can pick the right one for you:

 

  • Bank Iowa Checking
  • $ensible Checking
  • Interest Checking
  • Prestige Checking

 

While each account comes with different features to suit all personal finance shapes and sizes, there are a few standard ones that come with every Bank Iowa checking account:

 

  • No minimum opening deposit
  • Free online/mobile/text banking and bill pay
  • Tree-saving electronic statement option
  • Complimentary identity theft protection services

OVERDRAFT PRIVILEGE*

Bank Iowa understands mistakes can happen. That’s why we offer Overdraft Privilege for eligible personal checking accounts.

*Whether your overdraft will be paid is discretionary and we reserve the right to not pay. For example, we typically do not pay overdrafts if your account is not in good standing. Fee is $35. Repayment of fees and charges is due and payable upon demand. Categories of transactions covered include check, automatic bill payments, ATM transactions (if opted in), everyday debit card transactions (if opted in).

OVERDRAFT PROTECTION

In addition to our overdraft privilege, we also offer overdraft protection. Overdraft Protection is a line of credit tied directly to your personal checking account in an amount approved by a loan officer. Unlike Overdraft Privilege, when Overdraft Protection is used, you are not charged an Overdraft Fee (OD Fee) for each item that clears in excess of your current balance. Instead, the covering funds are applied to your checking account while the outstanding balance accrues interest until paid back to zero.
